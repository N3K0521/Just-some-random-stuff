Our Design Team

And Our Dream...

The three of us - Grace Gao, Anna Liao and Rebecca Wang have formed the team. We deeply loves this brand and our work. We are responsible for all matters such as design, pattern making and investment promotion. Our deep love to Razzbury has reduced the hardship of our business. Our dream is to let smart home come into every family, and free up housework time for more women in the new era, so that more of them can devote themselves to work and careers.

About Us

How We Develop

Razzbury is a young furniture designer brand, currently has more than 20 design products. The company's core value is "Let technology change life". We believe that AI technology and IoT concepts can open the era of intelligent life. As a young brand, we need the help of more investors. We are still working hard to make Razzbury an indispensable brand in the market. So far we have received 158 donations, totaling 10,000 Australian dollars. We have received 99% praise from more than 100 customized orders for smart wardrobes. We currently have studios in Melbourne and Perth and will come to Sydney in 2023.
